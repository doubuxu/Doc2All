Reverse stochastic process [Song 2021]

dxt = [f(t)xt - g2(t)∇x log qt(xt)]dt + g(t)dw̄t

can reverse the SDE [Anderson 1982]

dxt/dt = f(t)xt - 1/2 g2(t)∇x log qt(xt)

can also write as ODE [Maoutsa 2020]

Picard Iterations

Solve discretized ODE by iterating until convergence

Practical Issues

out of memory? Batching: process k timesteps at a time with sliding window

approximate? No measurable degradation on standard benchmarks
