Introduction

Training general-purpose large multimodal foundation models on purely sequential data, similar to linguistic inputs, has heralded a new frontier in visual perception. However, current endeavors are hamstrung by an over-reliance on colossal models, exemplified by the necessity for an extensive corpus of data, often comprising staggering 400B tokens. In this work, we delve into the development of an efficient, autoregression-based foundation model, innovatively architected to operate on a limited dataset.
