Background: Diffusion Schrödinger Bridge (SB)

Given two probability distributions p0, p1, how to transform p0 to p1 via a diffusion process and preserve the input-output similarity?

1. Formulation of the Schrödinger Bridge Problem: For two continuous distributions p0 and p1 on RD, is the Schrödinger bridge problem:

inf KL(P || Wε)

2. Structure of the Schrödinger Bridge: The Schrödinger bridge problem can be fully characterized by the initial distribution p0 and the Schrödinger potential φ*(x).

3. Integration of the Schrödinger Bridge SDE: The Schrödinger bridge SDE admits a closed-form solution expressed through the adjusted Schrödinger potential v*.

The minimizer T* is called the Schrödinger Bridge.

Proposed Algorithm: Light Schrödinger Bridge (LightSB)

Our solver is based on:

  • 1. Optimal parameterization of the Schrödinger bridge using mixtures of Gaussians.
  • 2. New loss function for training the Schrödinger bridge.
